Power-packed Features

iYMS is a complete and inexpensive tool to control the inventory of thousands of containers at a click of a button. It saves cost, time and increases efficiency in operation and productivity. iYMS enables its users to register all possible transactions for the whole life cycle of containers at their terminal i.e. Discharge, Gate-out, Gate-in, Maintenance & Repair etc.

The Booking Administration Module allows them to administer liner booking references and assign containers to the, when registering transactions. Users will be assisted with per container size/type data for each booking references and will be able to plan empty positioning together with depot's customers. iYMS is fully integrated with registration, load & discharge list functionality.
