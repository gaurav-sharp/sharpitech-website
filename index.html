The SAKSHAM Group - Building Bridges for the Future

Message from the Chairman's desk

As a leader, it has always been my vision & endeavour to nurture individuals within my organization to make them capable enough in adapting to consistent changes, thus making them sustainable as individuals as well as an organization as a whole. It is this drive that has led us, the management, to come up with “SAKSHAM”, as a group name to ensure fulfilment of this vision across among every individual, across various companies of the group, thus making the organization sustainable in the long term.

Education leads to awareness, awareness leads to enlightenment, enlightenment leads to the right path for a sustainable future. Thus, SAKSHAM is our initiative whereby every individual is educated to become capable enough to support one another thereby bringing sustainability in the organization as a whole.

- Ashish Sheth

Chairman

Core Values

Ability

At The SAKSHAM Group the management ensures that every individual is highly motivated enough to enhance their Abilities, training them to have the right attitude, thus becoming able enough to be a part of the organization having a goal of long term sustainability.

Adaptability

Change is the ONLY constant. Hence, at The SAKSHAM Group, adaptation to upcoming changes is not only encouraged but facilitated as once the inevitable is accepted, adaptability becomes effortless.

Sustainability

The Future depends on what we do in the present, and the best way to predict the future is to design it. Keeping this in mind, The SAKSHAM Group is focused on collaborating all its strengths and capabilities to tackle the doors of uncertainty, strengthen our resources to reflect who we are and ensure long term sustainability.

Philosophical inspiration

You are the master of your own destiny! You can influence, direct and control your own environment! You can make your life what you want it to be! You are the future!... ...The Future is YOU!

At the SAKSHAM Group, we believe that Capability gives rise to opportunities for Autonomy. Autonomy leads to Creativity and Creativity clubbed with Authority leads to the ability to deliver with Accountability, the fruit of which is Credibility and long term Sustainability.

Just as Atoms join together to form molecules, which in turn form compounds and universal chemicals, similarly, the Saksham Group comprises of individuals who together form companies and who in turn form “The SAKSHAM Group”, a stepping stone to construct a secure future for the whole organization and every individual working here. Our ambition is to shape the future for simpler accessibility to every function carried out with greater transparency and accountability leading to credibility and long term sustainability.
