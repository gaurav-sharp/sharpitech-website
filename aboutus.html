Who we are

Sharp iTech founded in 2008 is a part of Saksham group of company. The group is having global footprints over 80+ countries. The millions of hours of on-field experience in logistics counts. Still waiting? Join our digital ecosystem NOW.

Sharp iTech has been formed with very clear goal to improve the business processes efficiency of shipping and logistics domain. The foundation blocks are laid with the resolve to map, analyse, simply and redefine the processes to digitize and automate the system- reducing the data duplication- enabling the smart-data access routes and reducing the turn-around time per processes- hence, reducing overall logistics EXIM costs.

The idea was to give power to processes than people. The objective is to engage the exporters and importers in a seamless way providing errorless, in-time and the most economical way to execute the export/import orders. Our unique distinction is the vast expertise and experience, which 18+ years of on-field experience adorns us with.

Company’s flagship product iCMS, a web-based portal developed on niche technology stack with in-built AI and Machine learning capabilities. iCMS is a one-stop solution handling the entire shipping/ logistics ecosystem including the digital quote manager, sales negotiation, booking lifecycle manager, container movement tracking, yard manager, Inventory Plan and Movement manager, EXIM doc manager, Global Agency management. Legal claims manager, Revenue Management and auto-invoicing are unique toppings on an exquisite menu of our offerings.

Vision

To be the most preferred and reliable technology partner for 360-degree ecosystem of Logistics Industry around the globe. Let our IT innovations transform the way, we do logistics today.

Mission

Developing cloud-based, AI-ML enabled ecosystem of logistics which is simple, economical, adaptable, sustainable, scalable and responsive to customers’ core business needs.
