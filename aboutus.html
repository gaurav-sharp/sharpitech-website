Who we are

We believe in the idea of: Concept before implement, plan before develop. Our products and services narrate our ideology in array of horizontals and verticals, we serve.

SiT been formed with clear goal to improve technology and business process for Shipping and Logistics, to improve overall industry productivity, to bring transparency between various stakeholders, to avoid duplication of data capturing, to build a platform which can work 24 hours and round the clock, to reduce overall logistics Export and Import Cost and finally to engage with Exporters and Importers in a seamless way enabling errorless, in time and most economical way of executing their Export/Import orders/consignments. What differentiate SiT, is its functional experience of Shipping and Logistics of last more than 18+ years.

Company’s flagship product iCMS is web based portal develop on latest technology with inbuild AI and Machine learning capabilities. iCMS is an operating system or platform or echo system which can handle entire business cycle for movement of containers, procurement and service of ocean freight, execution of booking and documentation with shipping lines and also cross country transactions with overseas agencies. iCMS is has inbuilt features with auto invoicing and revenue management
